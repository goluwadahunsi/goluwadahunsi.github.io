HOW TO BECOME A TECH BRO

DIPLOMA/DEGREE

To become a tech bro via the conventional way is to attend a college or university to study either a diploma or bachelors in either Computer science/Engineering or Software Engineering. This is a good route, however if you don't have tutors or lecturers with industrial based approaches to tech, it will just become any other academic degree and you might have to substitute with extra courses elsehwere.

BOOTCAMPING

The second route is through the attendance of bootcamps which is like a compacted 12weeks to 30weeks accelerated course on software development and data science, they are usually tailored to meet your end points either to become a cyber security expert, data scientist, full-stack software developer etc. Bootcamps are good places, however you must do your home work in other to be able to find the best and affordable ones out there.

SELF-TAUGHT

The last but not the least is the "self-taught" route where you simulate your own learning modules and stick with it. This is the hardest and most tricky road as you might end up focusing on outdated concepts and algorithms, you can go to reddit or quora for a guide on how to start your journey if you really want to go via this route. I will however love to say this route does not make you a less tech bro than the other two routes.
